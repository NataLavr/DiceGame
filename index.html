Rules are simple

1

Objectives

The objective of the game is to obtain the highest score after three rolls of the dice. Each player takes turns rolling a pair of dice.On each turn, the player rolls both dice and calculates their score based on the scoring system provided. After three rolls, the player with the highest score wins the game.

2

Score

During each roll, the player's score is determined based on the outcome of the two dice. If either of the dice shows a 1, the player's score for that round is 0. If the two dice show the same number, the player's score is calculated by doubling the sum of the two dice. For any other combination of numbers, the player's score for that round is the sum of the two dice.

3

Rolls

The game progresses in a fixed manner with three rolls for each player. After each roll, the player immediately proceeds to the next roll without the option to stop or skip a turn. Once the third roll is completed, the player's score for that round is determined. This sequence continues until all players have completed their three rolls.
